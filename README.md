## 📱 Install as an App (PWA)
 
Beeran's Calculator Suite is a **Progressive Web App (PWA)** — this means you can install it directly from your browser and use it just like a native app, with no App Store required.
 
---
 
### Android (Chrome)
 
1. Open **Chrome** on your Android device
2. Go to **[beeranscalculatorsuite.com](https://beeranscalculatorsuite.com)**
3. Tap the **three-dot menu** (⋮) in the top-right corner
4. Tap **"Add to Home screen"**
5. Give it a name (or leave as-is) and tap **"Add"**
6. The app icon will appear on your home screen — tap it to launch
> The app opens fullscreen without the browser bar, just like a native app. It works completely offline after installation.
 
---
 
### iOS (Safari)
 
1. Open **Safari** on your iPhone or iPad
2. Go to **[beeranscalculatorsuite.com](https://beeranscalculatorsuite.com)**
3. Tap the **Share button** (the box with an arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the screen
4. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
5. Give it a name and tap **"Add"** in the top-right corner
6. The app icon will appear on your home screen — tap it to launch
> **Note:** PWA installation on iOS requires Safari. Chrome and other browsers on iOS do not support "Add to Home Screen" for PWAs.
 
---
 
### Windows / Mac (Chrome or Edge)
 
1. Open **Chrome** or **Edge** on your computer
2. Go to **[beeranscalculatorsuite.com](https://beeranscalculatorsuite.com)**
3. Look for the **install icon** (⊕ or a small computer icon) in the browser's address bar, on the right side
4. Click it and select **"Install"**
5. The app opens in its own window without browser controls
> If you don't see the install icon, click the browser menu and look for **"Install Beeran's Calculator Suite"** or **"Save and share → Install page as app"**.

## 🔄 Keeping It Updated
 
The app updates automatically in the background. If you ever want to force a refresh to get the latest version:
 
- **Android / Desktop:** Hold `Ctrl` (or `Cmd` on Mac) and press `Shift + R` in the browser, or clear the app's site data in your browser settings.
- **iOS:** Delete the app from your home screen and re-add it from Safari.
